ABOUT US

Welcome to the Library LINK — a user-friendly platform designed to streamline library services for both students and staff. This system was developed with the goal of enhancing the efficiency of book lending, tracking, and inventory management in academic institutions.

We developed this website as part of our Information Systems (IS) Elective course. This project serves as our final requirement, encouraging us to use the skills and knowledge we obtained over the semester.

Our goal was to produce a functioning, user-friendly platform for school-related use. From strategy to coding and design, we worked together as a team to create this website with intention and creativity. It reflects our learning experiences in web development, user interface design, and system functionality.

TERMS & CONDITIONS

Last updated: July 1, 2026

1. Acceptance of Terms

By creating an account or using Library Link, you agree to these Terms & Conditions. If you do not agree, please do not register or use the borrowing features of the platform.

2. Eligibility

You must be at least 13 years old to create an account. By registering, you confirm that the information you provide, including your birthday, is accurate.

3. Account Responsibility

You are responsible for keeping your password confidential and for all activity that happens under your account. Notify us right away if you suspect unauthorized access.

4. Borrowing Policy

Members may borrow up to one book per category, with a maximum of three books checked out at a time. Borrowed books are due 14 days after checkout, and availability is shown live on each title.

5. Late Fees & Lost Items

Late fees are charged at $0.25 per day per title, capped at $5.00 per book. Lost or significantly damaged items may be charged at replacement cost, as described in our Announcements.

6. Acceptable Use

You agree not to misuse the platform — including attempting to bypass borrowing limits, submitting false account information, or interfering with other members' access to the catalog.

7. Privacy

Information you provide, such as your name, email, gender, and birthday, is used only to operate your account and library services. We do not sell member information to third parties.

8. Changes to These Terms

We may update these Terms & Conditions from time to time. Continued use of Library Link after changes are posted means you accept the revised terms.

9. Contact Us

Questions about these terms can be sent through the Contact Us link in the footer.

PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: July 1, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what information Library Link collects, how it's used, and the choices you have. By using the platform, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, gender, birthday, and password. We also keep a record of your borrowing activity, saved books, and notification history so the platform can work correctly.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to manage your account, track borrowed and returned books, calculate due dates and late fees, personalize your homepage, and send relevant notifications and announcements.

4. Data Storage

Account and activity data is stored only for as long as your account is active. You may request account deletion at any time through the Contact Us page.

5. Cookies & Local Data

Library Link may use basic session data to keep you signed in while you browse. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Information may only be shared when required by law or to protect the security of the platform.

7. Your Rights

You can view and update your name, gender, and birthday anytime from My Profile. You may also request a copy of your data or ask us to delete your account.

8. Children's Privacy

Library Link requires members to be at least 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under this age.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Library Link after changes are posted means you accept the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

Questions about how your data is handled can be sent through the Contact Us link in the footer.
